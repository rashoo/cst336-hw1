The Linux OS is an open-source software. It can be used for free, and advanced users are welcomed to help improve it. Linux OS can be found on multiple types of computers, including laptops, desktops, mobile phones and high-performance servers. Linux OS is not typically found on consumer laptops, but it has heavily influenced Android OS and billions of people that use it around the world. Most major techonology company uses Linux OS on thier severs for performance and security.
